*¡Hola! Keila is now available in Spanish – making it even easier for Spanish-speaking users to manage their newsletters.*

<KeilaImage
  src="/src/assets/updates/2025-12-26-spanish-translation.png"
  alt="Screenshot of the Keila interface in Spanish."
></KeilaImage>

Thanks to a community contribution, the Keila interface is now fully available in Spanish.
This brings the total number of supported languages to four: English, German, French, and Spanish.

Want to use Keila in your own language? There is a short [technical translation guide on GitHub](https://github.com/pentacent/keila/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#translating-keila).
If you would like to contribute and need help getting started, please don't hesitate to reach out on GitHub
or send an email to <Email user="hello" domain="keila.io" />.
