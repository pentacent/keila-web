_Customize the alignment of text in your email campaigns with the new text alignment options._

<KeilaImage

The Block Editor now allows you to choose the alignment of text in your email campaigns. You can align text to the left, right, center, or justify it. This gives you more control over the appearance of your emails and helps you create visually appealing content.
