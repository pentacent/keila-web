**Keila Cloud now includes a new Partner Mode designed for agencies and freelancers who need to manage multiple sub-accounts for clients. Clients get full access to the features of Keila Cloud without needing their own subscription.**


<KeilaImage
  src="/src/assets/updates/2026-05-16-partner-mode.png"
  alt="Screenshot showing the Partner Mode interface."
  maxWidth="640"
></KeilaImage>

With Partner Mode, you can use a single Keila Cloud subscription and manage multiple sub-accounts for your clients. This allows you to keep a single billing account and take ownership of the billing relationship with your clients.

As a partner, you can create and manage sub-accounts. Once you've been approved as a partner, your sub-accounts are activated automatically and don't need to go through our usual verification process.

<KeilaImage
  src="/src/assets/updates/2026-05-16-partner-mode-create-account.png"
  alt="Screenshot showing a new sub-account being created in Partner Mode."
  maxWidth="640"
></KeilaImage>

You can also transfer credits from your account to sub-accounts: You can transfer them manually or set up automatic monthly credit allocations.
