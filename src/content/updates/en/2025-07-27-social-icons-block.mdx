_Easily add social media links to your email campaigns with the new social icons block._

<KeilaImage
  src="/src/assets/updates/2025-07-27-social-icons-block.png"
  alt="Screenshot showing the social media icons block in the Keila Block Editor"
/>

We've added a new social media icons block to the Block Editor. This makes it easy to include links to your social media profiles in your email campaigns. The social media icons block allows you to change the color and the size of the icons.

## Supported Networks

The block comes with built-in support for 20 popular platforms:

- Mastodon
- Bluesky
- Discord
- Discourse
- Facebook
- GitHub
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- Reddit
- Signal
- Slack
- Snapchat
- SoundCloud
- Threads
- TikTok
- Twitch
- WhatsApp
- X (Twitter)
- Xing
- YouTube

Need a platform that's not on the list? You can also add custom icons and colors.
